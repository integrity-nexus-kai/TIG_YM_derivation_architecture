# Open Questions

## Purpose

This document records the current unresolved mathematical and geometric questions within the TIG3 admissibility program.

The purpose of this file is organizational.

The listed questions are not weaknesses of the framework.

They define the active research frontier of TIG3.

---

# Foundational Status

TIG3 currently provides:

- a structurally defined branch framework,
- admissibility conditions,
- bounded-curvature targets,
- GR consistency constraints,
- and a preliminary proof architecture.

However, several major mathematical problems remain open.

---

# Core Open Questions

## 1. Covariant Field Structure

A complete covariant TIG3 field equation has not yet been established.

Open problems include:

- admissible tensor structure,
- geometric source terms,
- compatibility with branch admissibility,
- and stability of regulated sectors.

This is currently the central mathematical gap.

---

## 2. Dynamical Evolution

The current TIG3 framework lacks a complete dynamical evolution equation.

Open questions include:

- nonlinear branch evolution,
- global continuation behavior,
- stability under perturbation,
- and long-time asymptotics.

No PDE control currently exists.

---

## 3. Curvature Control

Although bounded-curvature sectors are hypothesized, no rigorous curvature theorem has been proven.

Open problems include:

- invariant estimates,
- near-critical curvature bounds,
- and admissible continuation criteria.

---

## 4. Near-Critical Stability

The regime

beta ≈ beta_c

remains mathematically poorly understood.

Open questions include:

- metastable dynamics,
- relaxation behavior,
- spectral structure,
- and branch sensitivity.

Current understanding is primarily heuristic.

---

## 5. Global Geometry

The global topology of admissible TIG3 vacuum sectors remains unknown.

Open questions include:

- causal structure,
- global extendability,
- admissible topology classes,
- and horizon-sector continuation.

---

## 6. Simulation Consistency

The current TIG3 simulations remain heuristic.

Missing components include:

- covariant evolution,
- numerical convergence analysis,
- stability verification,
- and invariant tracking.

---

## 7. Observational Structure

No observational program currently exists.

Open questions include:

- observable near-critical signatures,
- photon-sphere deviations,
- relaxation spectra,
- and gravitational-wave effects.

These questions remain secondary to the mathematical program.
