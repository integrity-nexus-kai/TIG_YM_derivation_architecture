# Vacuum Admissibility Conditions

The TIG3 vacuum sector investigates which geometric configurations remain structurally admissible under the TIG2 branch constraints.

Primary admissibility targets:

- finite curvature structure,
- branch stability,
- regular continuation,
- asymptotic consistency,
- topology compatibility,
- non-singular critical behavior.

The purpose of this file is to collect exact admissibility conditions as they are rigorously derived.
