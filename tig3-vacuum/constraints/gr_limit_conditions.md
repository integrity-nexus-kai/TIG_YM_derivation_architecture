# GR Limit Conditions

## Purpose

This document defines the general-relativistic consistency requirements imposed on the TIG3 vacuum sector.

## Core Principle

TIG3 must recover standard general relativity in the appropriate weak-structure and asymptotic limits.

## Minimal Conditions

1. Schwarzschild recovery for beta -> 0

2. Standard asymptotic behavior at large radial distance

3. Absence of spurious long-range deviations

4. Compatibility with classical horizon structure away from the critical branch sector

5. Continuous reduction to admissible GR geometries outside the regulated core region

## Remarks

The GR limit is treated as a non-negotiable structural consistency condition of the TIG framework.
