# TIG3 Research Architecture

This document defines the formal research architecture of the TIG3 vacuum-sector program.

The TIG3 objective is the derivation of admissible vacuum structures compatible with the TIG2 horizon branch sector.

The research program is constrained by:

- exact derivations,
- structural admissibility,
- discriminant analysis,
- stability theory,
- topology,
- asymptotic consistency,
- preservation of the GR limit.

The architecture separates:

1. definitions,
2. admissibility constraints,
3. proof structure,
4. computational checks,
5. observational outlooks,
6. conjectural extensions.

No statement in TIG3 is to be interpreted as established unless explicitly derived from stated assumptions.
