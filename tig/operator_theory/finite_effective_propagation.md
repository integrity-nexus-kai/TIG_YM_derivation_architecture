# Purpose

This document defines the hypothesis that admissible TIG configurations preserve finite effective asymptotic propagation near critical infrared-compatible regimes.

The objective is to characterize bounded admissible long-range structural evolution.

\[

Without admissibility constraints, this may induce:

An admissible TIG configuration preserves finite effective asymptotic propagation.

- unstable asymptotic amplification is dynamically suppressed,
- and structurally divergent propagation sectors are excluded.
