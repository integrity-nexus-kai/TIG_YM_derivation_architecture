# Purpose

This document defines the hypothesis that admissible TIG configurations may induce effective structural hierarchy between stable admissible low-energy sectors and asymptotically unstable high-instability sectors.

The objective is to characterize bounded admissible asymptotic energy organization.

An admissible TIG configuration may preserve an effective asymptotic hierarchy between:

- admissible bounded low-energy sectors,
- and non-admissible unstable asymptotic sectors.

- stable asymptotic propagation remains bounded,
- structurally unstable sectors become dynamically excluded,
- and infrared-compatible evolution remains restricted.
