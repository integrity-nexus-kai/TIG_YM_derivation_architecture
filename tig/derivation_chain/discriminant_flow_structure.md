\[

\[
\Delta=0,

\[

# Structural Interpretation

Discriminant flow currently refers only to:

- change of branch separation,
- critical accessibility reduction,
- and admissibility-compatible compression.

It does NOT imply:

- physical time evolution,
- dynamical field flow,
- renormalization-group flow,
- or quantum evolution.
