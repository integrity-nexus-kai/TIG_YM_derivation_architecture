# Purpose

This document defines the canonical registry of unresolved mathematical and structural problems within the current TIG framework.

Its purpose is to preserve explicit rigor boundaries and prevent unresolved structures from being misrepresented as completed results.

---

# Core Principle

Within TIG:

- unresolved problems must remain explicit,
- incompleteness must remain visible,
- and structural limitations must not be hidden.

Open problems are considered an essential component of mathematical rigor.
