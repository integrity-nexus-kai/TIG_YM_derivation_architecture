# 5. Consistency Guidance

Inside canonical operator documents, it is generally preferable to avoid:

```text
ultimate theory
theory of everything
final vacuum structure
universal intelligence
undefined higher-dimensional terminology
```

The preferred style remains:

- operator-theoretic,
- functional-analytic,
- spectral,
- geometrically controlled,
- mathematically conservative.

# 6. Yang--Mills Language Guidance

Inside exploratory operator documents, the following formulations should generally be avoided unless rigorously established:

```text

TIG3 proves the Mass Gap
TIG3 proves confinement
TIG3 defines the Yang--Mills Hamiltonian
```

Preferred alternatives include:

```text

spectral stabilization mechanisms
```
